Professional Profile

Capital markets practitioner.
Investment decision-maker.

Charles Gamo has spent over three decades at the intersection of capital allocation, strategic advisory, and governance — originating and executing transactions, evaluating and managing investments, and advising boards and executives across the Philippines' most demanding industries.

His experience spans both sides of the capital table: as a banker originating and structuring transactions for issuers, and as a principal screening, underwriting, and overseeing direct investments across growth-stage and established businesses in healthcare, fintech, edtech, energy, and digital infrastructure.

As a Certified Executive Coach, Charles also works with senior executives and founder teams to sharpen strategic clarity, governance discipline, and investment readiness — making him equally effective in deal origination, portfolio stewardship, and board oversight roles.
